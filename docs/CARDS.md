# Structural Notes

- Brown and Dark Blue require 2 cards to complete.
- Railroads require 4 cards.
- Utilities require 2 cards.
- All other color sets require 3 cards.
- House adds +$3M rent to a full set.
- Hotel adds +$4M rent to a set with a House.
- Action cards and Wildcards may be banked as money.
